  • DSCA Circuit (Fastest Finger First)

    In this project, our team undertook the exciting challenge of creating a circuit with a festive touch, specifically tailored for Diwali celebrations. The result? "Fastest Finger First," an engaging game featuring four push buttons and corresponding LEDs. The concept is simple yet thrilling: the participant who rapidly presses a push button will illuminate the associated LED, fostering a competitive and lively atmosphere. With the capability for up to three players to participate simultaneously, the game adds a dynamic and social element to the festivities. To reset the game and prepare for subsequent rounds, an additional push button is incorporated, resetting all buttons, LEDs, and activating a buzzer for a seamless and continuous gaming experience.
